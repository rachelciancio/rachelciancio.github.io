Insurance

I accept most major insurances as both an in-network and out-of-network provider.

Services may be covered in full or in part by your health insurance or Employee Assistance Plan. Please check your coverage carefully by asking the following questions:

  • Do I have mental health insurance benefits?
  • What is my deductible and has it been met?
  • How many sessions per year does my health insurance cover
  • What is the coverage amount per therapy session?
  • Is approval required from my primary care physician?
